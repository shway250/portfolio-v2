Jackson Duhon is a Seattle based full-stack web developer and digital artist. In 2015 Jackson received a degree from the Music Technology, Interaction, Intelligence, and Design program at the California Institute of the Arts, which is where he first discovered his love of coding and being surrounded by creative weirdos. Jackson recently finished a web development immersive course at General Assembly and is looking for new opportunities in the Seattle tech sector. Jackson's a pretty cool dude, everyone says so.
